Current use of AI:

Although artificial intelligence evokes science fiction, artificial intelligence has many uses today , for example:

  1. Email filtering: Email services use artificial intelligence to filter incoming e-mails.
    Users can train their spam filters by marking emails as spam filters.

  2. Personalization: Online services make use of artificial intelligence to personalize your experience.
    Services, such as Amazon or Netflix, "learn" from your previous purchases and purchases by other users in order to recommend relevant content to you.

  3. Fraud detection: Banks use artificial intelligence to determine if there is an odd activity on your account.
    Unexpected activities, such as foreign transactions, could be flagged by an algorithm.

  4. Speech Recognition: Use artificial intelligence to optimize speech recognition functions.
    Examples include intelligent personal assistants, for example. "Alexa" from Amazon or "Siri" from Apple.
