AI has mutiple applications on a lot of fields and its usage is increasing rapidly.
As time passes artificial intelligence is becoming more effective
and more powerful and that makes it more usable
in different applications
such as:-

  • Artificial business intelligence:
    Robotic process automation is applied to highly repetitive tasks normally performed by humans.
    Machine learning algorithms are integrated into analytics and Customer Relationship Management
    platforms to uncover information on how to better serve clients. Chatbots have already been incorporated
    into websites and e-companies to provide immediate service to customers. Automation of employment positions
    has also become a point of discussion between academics and IT consultants.

  • AI in education:
    it automates grading, giving more time to educators.
    It can also assess and adjust students to their needs , allowing them to function at their own pace.

  • AI for robotics:
    will allow us to face the needs of the elderly and to encourage a much longer independence.
    It will reduce significantly, also decrease road accidents and deaths and encourage disasters
    to respond to threats, e.g. the nuclear meltdown of the Fukushima plant.
